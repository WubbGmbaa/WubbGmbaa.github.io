During my senior year in high school, a friend and I started a club with the goal of building an arcade machine. Over the course of three months, we led a team of students through the process of building a MAME cabinet almost completely from scratch. The machine is now an entertainment staple in between classes, and makes occasional appearances at school events.

Originally, the cabinet ran on a decommissioned server (trash-picked from the IT department), modified to use a GTX 670 FTW graphics card (donated by one of my peers). To solve power consumption issues, these were replaced with a spare laptop in early 2019. I adopted the server for personal use. (Incidentally, Toan, if you ever read this, I still have your GPU. I've lost your number - contact me.)
